Inspiration

This started out as a small project at Gray Area foundation of the Arts Immersive showcase. This was a small inkling into a much larger independent research topic while making at UC Davis Center for Design and ViDi Labs. My first time "drawing" what playing the piano looked like in realtime felt magical. I thought if only I can make a journal of all of these captures. Then I'd have something worthwhile.

Implementation

Processing canvas sketch run by accelerometer data mapping xyz axis points. Mqtt on a local wifi server to send and recieve data and to implement wireless access to a wristband for portability.

Futures

The ideology of mapping has influenced emerging technology that we use every day. There are syneasthetic qualities in most of our everyday activity then mapped to a value of information. But the meaningfullness behind this is that this influences perceptual influences on anthropology and our ecological environment.
