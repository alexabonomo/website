Implementation

Processing drew on to a canvas using spatial input from a kinect ir sensor. Within processing, the ir sensor took a bitmapped grid of pixels, mapped a retangle around the max distances on the x and y axis and calculated the center to draw a point, thus forming lines segments from one vector to the next. The bit map mirroring a person showed up as grey and the lines were colored by a spectrum class that preformed a function to iterate over the hsb spectrum.
