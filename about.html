Alexa Ann Bonomo is a creative technologist based in Davis, CA. Alexa moved to the Bay Area in 2017 to pursue her practice and continue her education through Gray Area Foundation for the Arts and Technology. She has since shown at various places in the Bay Area including Kala Art Institute, The Grand Theater, and the Exploratorium. Currently, Alexa is working towards an MFA in Design at the University of California, Davis to research experiential methods in data visualization and is in collaboration with the UC Davis Center for Data Visualization and Interface Innovation. Her research disects and formulates data driven ecosystems that are embedded within personal surroundings.
