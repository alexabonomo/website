I use programming as a creative way to create and discover new possibilities in the intersection of design and technology. Currently, I am a graduate student at University of California Davis working on my MFA in design as well as working as a teaching assistant for undergraduate courses. I am incredibly interested in studying interactive data visualization.

I have shown large and small scale works in San Francisco at The Exploratorium and Gray Area Foundation for the Arts and more recently at Jan Shrem and Maria Manetti Shrem Museum of Art. Going forward, I am working on discovering new possibilities in web based applications, data mining, and incorporation of machine learning to enhance data visualizations.
